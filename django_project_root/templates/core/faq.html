Frequently Asked Questions

Can I schedule a call with your team?

Yes! Simply mention your preferred time in the message form, and we'll reach out to schedule a convenient time for both parties.

Currently, we provide support in English. However, we're working on expanding our language support to serve our global community better.

Where can I find your help center?

Visit our comprehensive Help Center for guides, tutorials, and answers to common questions. You can access it from the footer of any page.
